Alan Turing

Shortened WWII by 2 Years, Saving 14+ Million Lives


A timeline of Alan Turing's life and his legacy:

  • 1912: Alan Turing was born in Maida Vale, London, England on June 23rd, 1912
  • 1934: Turing graduates in Mathematics from King's College, located in Cambridge.
  • 1938: Obtains a PhD. in Mathematics at Princeton University
  • 1939: Joins the Government Codes and Ciphers and arrives at Bletchley Park one day after World War II was declared.
